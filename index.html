Concept

Embark on a fun journey with Bao Bao! Earn "Golden Pastry Points" with every purchase, level up your status, and unlock exclusive rewards along the way.

How It Works

  • Scan & Earn: Every purchase earns you Golden Pastry Points 🥮
  • Level Up: Start as a Pastry Apprentice and rise to Master Baker by collecting points
  • Mini Games: Enjoy simple in-app games (like catching flying pastries or mixing ingredients) for bonus rewards 🎮
  • Bao Bao Surprises: Receive tips, encouragement, or surprise gifts from Bao Bao 🎩

Reward System

Tier Points Needed Reward
🍪 Pastry Apprentice 50 pts Free mini pastry
🎩 Dough Master 150 pts Exclusive Bao Bao sticker
🥮 Golden Baker 300 pts 10% off next purchase
⭐ Master Baker 500 pts Limited-edition Hang Heung gift 🎁

Bonus Rewards

  • Surprise Birthday Gift 🎂
  • Referral Rewards: Invite a friend and both receive bonus points!
  • Bao Bao’s Challenge: Limited-time seasonal challenges (e.g., a Mid-Autumn pastry hunt)

Implementation

  • No app download required! Works via WeChat, WhatsApp, or Web QR Code
  • Quick Scan System: Customers scan at checkout to collect points
  • No physical cards – all points are stored digitally
  • Real-time progress dashboard showing your points and level

About

This project introduces Bao Bao’s Bakery Quest, a cafe loyalty program designed to engage customers with fun challenges and rewards.
